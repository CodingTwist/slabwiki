## The basics

1. **Be decent.** No harassment, hate speech, or being a nuisance to others.
2. **No griefing or stealing.** Don't break or take what isn't yours.
3. **No cheating.** No hacked clients, x-ray, duping, or exploits.
4. **Keep it PG-13.** Builds and chat stay family-friendly.
5. **Ask before major public changes.** Coordinate on shared/community builds.

_Full, current rules live on [slabserver.org](https://slabserver.org/)._
