The Slab Edition's whole reason for existing is to remove the singleplayer
waiting problem from the original Hermitcraft build: multiple players can be
inside their own dungeon instances at the same time.

The key design guarantee is that **only the setup is bespoke** - the plot board,
the Shulker Deck, the card system and the trade barrels are the custom shell.
Once you cross the entrance, *"Everything after you enter the instance is vanilla
and exactly how the game plays in survival mode."* Difficulty comes entirely
from vanilla mechanics amplified by clank, soul flames and your equipped cards -
not from server-side scripting inside the run.
