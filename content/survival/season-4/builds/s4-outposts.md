Season 4 started with a boarder of 4K blocks on each extreme of the world. Outposts are made with Ender Pearl restock stations and general utilities to reach the resource world!

###  Moss Outpost

![Image of the moss outpost](S4Moss_Outpost.png "Image of the moss outpost") The moss outpost was designed by . The outpost was positioned underground due to being in an ocean; luckily we found a cave with a nice opening to throw a pearl in. With some terraforming the cave was converted into mystical underground cave. There is glowing amethysts, leaves and diamonds lining the wall.
