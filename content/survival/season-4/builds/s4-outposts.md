Season 4 started with a boarder of 4K blocks on each extreme of the world. Outposts are made with {{< mcicon "enderpearl" >}} Ender Pearl restock stations and general utilities to reach the resource world!

### {{< mcicon "mossblock" >}} Moss Outpost



![Image of the moss outpost](/images/articles/s4moss-outpost.png "Image of the moss outpost") The moss outpost was designed by {{< playerhead "GamingTwist" >}}. The outpost was positioned underground due to being in an ocean; luckily we found a cave with a nice opening to throw a pearl in. With some terraforming the cave was converted into mystical underground cave. There is glowing amethysts, leaves and diamonds lining the wall.

### {{< mcicon "ice" >}} Ice Outpost



![Image of the ice outpost](/images/articles/s4-ice-outpost.png "Image of the ice outpost") The ice outpost was designed by {{< playerhead "Etho" >}}.

It was redesigned by {{< playerhead "Tonacane" >}}.

*Etho Plays Minecraft - Episode 106* was the episode where the iconic creeper face was made. That landmark now stands as the portal slabbers use to access the resource world from the Ice Outpost.
