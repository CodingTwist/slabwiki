The Passage was a puzzle created by {{< playerhead "DaMarine" >}}, {{< playerhead "chrissamael" >}}, and {{< playerhead "marbou9" >}} for Season 4. Designed around uncovering the mystery of a vast interconnected network of caves, caverns, and tunnels that descend impossibly far underground, the puzzle was implemented using both datapacks and plugins. It serves as the conclusion to the spiritual trilogy of "[The Disc](/survival/season-2/the-disc-11-puzzle/)" and "[The Tunnel](/survival/season-3/the-tunnel/)" puzzles from Seasons 2 and 3, once again requiring collaboration by the Slabserver community in order to solve.
