## What is Decked Out?

Decked Out is inspired by the game of the same name created by TangoTek on the HermitCraft server.

In Decked Out, players enter a custom dungeon filled with ravagers and need to deliver a compass to its destination. While in the dungeon, players can pursue various other goals, such as soul flames, loot chests, lore books, and more.

If the player survives the dungeon, they get several rewards. First, they can pick between 1 of 3 cards to add to their deck, each card adding a different effect to the player's dungeon run. Next, the player receives several artifacts of various rarities which can be collected or traded with other players. When a player collects a complete set of artifacts, they can be turned in for a point. The first player to get to 15 points wins decked out.
