Twisties is a shop created by GamingTwist, designed as a redstone mechanism that allows users to purchase ice in a way similar to buying ice cream from a real-life van.
The redstone system operates by relocating the logic beneath the shop. Twist had to perform intricate compacting to fit a shulker display, four inputs, stock lights, and a five-block type swapper all within the van.
