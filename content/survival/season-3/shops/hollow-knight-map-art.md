Hollow Knight Map Art was a shop built towards the end of S3. It sold, as the name suggests, map art of Queen's Gardens, lush green area from the game Hollow Knight.

Designed by {{< playerhead "Vileoss" >}}, the shop building resembled the architectural style of Hallownest. Above ground was the typical tall building with its iconic bench, where you could rest your legs before continuing downstairs. As you entered the basement, a noteblock cover of the Green Path theme started playing and the overgrown garden revealed itself. You then followed the path, stepped through the map art and found a hidden grub offering you his wares.

At 144 maps, the 16x9 map art was the largest piece made on S3. It took Kiiwi over 8 months to finish and stayed secret for most of the season, only revealed to the public during the final year. It was first featured as the first place prize for the Dragon Building Competition hosted by Kiiwi, and then sold as regular map art for 4 stacks of diamond blocks each. Slabbers had the option to choose either the normal version or the puzzle version, where the map pieces were shuffled randomly inside the shulkers. Total of 4 pieces were sold and the earned diamond blocks were displayed in diamond mountains behind the shop.

A meme version of the map art was made by {{< playerhead "GamingTwist" >}}, {{< playerhead "BlahBlah81" >}}, {{< playerhead "ReveredOxygen" >}}, {{< playerhead "xijtsjis" >}} and {{< playerhead "eipeoderemenosmw" >}}, and was sold in the Illegal Shop.

The Hollow Knight Map Art shop won a Slabby for the best S3 shop at the end of the season.
